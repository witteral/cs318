Who Are We?

At the Event Production Crew, we believe in working together to reach our goals and empowering each other to be our best. Together we work to put on every single event that happens on campus, and we couldn't do that without student employees who go the extra mile to learn technical skills such as lighting, audio, and video production, and more. Here you will find resources for training on these technical subjects so that you can advance your skills and learn to run events.

Event Services student staff goes through extensive training sessions each semester covering general procedures, safety, and specialized knowledge for their unique duties on campus. Each team also has a student supervisor that works as a team leader and coordinates with each team's respective staff supervisor.Together we work to put on the best events we can every day of the year.

What Will This Do For You?

Pursuing technical training with us will benefit you both now and after you graduate. Completing these trainings will teach you important life skills such as dedication, patience, team work, creative design, and much more. Working for the betterment of this team will benefit you in enumerable ways. Not only will you be able to run high-profile concerts and shows, but you will be able to take pride in the fact that you worked to be a part of something bigger than yourself and that you were able to leave your mark.

Pursuing these trainings with the Event Production Crew will allow you to become more involved with your school, with the community, and with your fellow employees. It is truly an experience you won't be able to find anywhere else on campus.
